FAQ

Quick answers about Colours & Patterns.

Where is Colours & Patterns located?

Colours & Patterns is headquartered in Guwahati, Assam. We serve clients across Northeast India, including Dibrugarh, Tezpur, Silchar, Shillong, Dimapur, and Imphal.

When was Colours & Patterns founded?

Colours & Patterns was founded in 2017 by Isha Poddar Agarwal in Guwahati, Assam.

What services does Colours & Patterns offer?

We offer brand strategy and identity, social media marketing, performance advertising, website development, verified WhatsApp API marketing, and content and creative production.

Which industries does Colours & Patterns serve?

We work with automobile dealerships, real estate and construction firms, hospitality and F&B brands, retail and lifestyle businesses, healthcare and education organisations, and corporate brands entering the Northeast.

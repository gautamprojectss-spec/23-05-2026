Service 07 — Wedding Design

Wedding Design.

Premium creative for weddings and celebrations. Colours & Patterns brings the same design discipline to invitations, save-the-dates, signage, and the full visual identity of the day — so every celebration looks coordinated and beautifully on brand.

Discuss This Service All Services

What this service covers

One design language, from the invite to the venue.

A wedding is a brand for a day — and it deserves the same care. We design a single, elegant visual identity for the celebration, then carry it across every printed and digital touchpoint so the whole day feels intentional and complete.

What we do

  • Wedding identity, monogram, colour palette, and motif design
  • Invitations, save-the-dates, digital invites, and RSVP cards
  • Signage, seating, menus, welcome boards, and venue branding

Deliverables

  • Wedding logo
  • Invitations
  • Digital invite
  • Signage
  • Menus
  • Welcome board

Built for celebrations that want to feel coordinated.

  • Couples planning a themed celebration with a distinct identity

  • Premium venues and banquets presenting a coordinated guest experience

Agency-grade design, made personal.
